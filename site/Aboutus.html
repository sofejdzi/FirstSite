Welcome to my first project!


Hello!
My name is Sofija Stolić and currently I'm a first grade student of Internet Technologies
at the ICT College of Vocational studies in Belgrade, Serbia (©Visoka ICT škola).

LOVE YOURSELF is my very first school project, so I've taken some references
from other sites (you can see some similarities). I chose this topic because I love makeup
and I thought I'll have fun while making a web-site about something that holds my interest in real life.

It's very difficult to find custom made foundation in Serbia
so I thought it would be a great idea to bring in and introduce this product to my country.
