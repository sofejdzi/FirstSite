LY Liquid Foundation


Our hyper-intelligent technology takes five skin scans, analyzes your skintone,
and then blends a perfectly matched all-in-one foundation just for you.
Each bottle is personalized with your name and the date that it was made.

We suggest scanning at least two to four times a year
to capture your skin's ever-changing tone from season to season.



Step 1

'Love Yourself' Color Expert scans your skin to measure your exact shade.

Step 2

Our comprehensive diagnostic calculates your unique formula by selecting your skin type and coverage level.

Step 3

Your custom foundation is prepared right in front of you. A few minutes to mix and it's ready.

Step 4

Your name and complexion ID are also labeled on the bottle.
